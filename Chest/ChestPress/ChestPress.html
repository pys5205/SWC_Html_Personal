Chest Press Machine

Place your hips and upper back on a bench and open your chest as much as possible.
Tighten your chest and push forward.
While keeping your wrists and elbows in a straight line, slowly pull your elbows back.
Pec Deck Fly Machine

After adjusting the chair height of the machine so that the arms and elbows are in the center of the chest, adjust the machine arm angle so that it is in line with your shoulders without tilting the machine arm too far back.
When sitting on a chair, attach the upper back and buttocks to the chair and make the waist slightly arched with the rib cage widened so that the chest is relaxed.
After holding it well so that your posture is not disturbed, bring your arms to the inside of your chest.
