Babel Chest Exercise 한국어
Barbell Bench Press Incline Barbell Bench Press Floor Press
Barbell Bench Press

Lay flat on the bench with your feet on the ground. With straight arms unrack the bar.
Lower the bar to your mid chest.
Raise the bar until you've locked your elbows.
Incline Barbell Bench Press

Position the bench between 30 and 45 degrees.
Lay flat on the bench with your feet on the ground. With straight arms unrack the bar.
Lower the bar to your mid chest
Raise the bar (slowly and controlled) until you've locked your elbows.
Floor Press

Un-rack the bar and break at the elbows and shoulders.
Bring your elbows to the floor, rest for a second to remove all the momentum from the rep, then press to a lockout.
