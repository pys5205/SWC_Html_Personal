Dumbbel Chest Exercise 한국어
Dumbbel Press Dumbbel Pull Over Dumbbell Fly
Dumbbel Press

Hold dumbbells and arch your back Both legs are firmly anchored to the floor.
Dumbbells are placed between the center of the chest and the armpits and Bend your elbows slightly and spread your arms shoulder-width apart.
Fix your gaze with dumbbells and raise it slowly and Raise the dumbbells faster than when lowering them.
Slowly, maximizing the relaxation of your chest muscles, lower your chest until your arms are parallel.
Dumbbel Pull Over

Lie on a bench with your back arched.
Lift the dumbbells up so that they are perpendicular to your chest.
With your elbows slightly bent, slowly lower your arms to the top of your head.
Slowly draw a parabola so that the stimulation of the chest muscles and back can go, return to the original position and repeat.
Dumbbell Fly

Fix your gaze with dumbbells and raise it so that it can be positioned above your chest.
Slowly open your arms and lower the dumbbells to the chest position.
Return to the starting position in a semicircle.
